03
Two Different Battles

Organic search and the Google Map Pack are two separate fights.

This is the single most important strategic distinction in the whole report. Different rules, different timelines, different opponents. Your website tackles one of them directly and sets up the long game on the other.

I.

Organic Search

The blue links on Google's results page. Won through the website.

  • Rewards: page architecture, on-page SEO, content depth, local signals, schema markup, internal linking
  • Does NOT require: decades of reviews, decades in business, massive ad spend
  • Why we can win it fast: only Long Heating is defending Greenville keywords — no one is defending Easley, Greer, or Seneca
  • Typical timeline: 90–180 days for satellite cities, 6–12 months for Greenville flagship terms
THE TAKEAWAY
Your website rebuild wins this battle directly. The 11-page architecture targets keywords no competitor is defending at the dedicated-page level.
II.

The Map Pack

The three businesses with map pins at the top of search results. Won through reputation.

  • Rewards: Google Business Profile completeness, review count + velocity, GBP age, proximity to searcher
  • Does NOT primarily reward: website on-page SEO, blog content (those help indirectly)
  • Why it takes longer: incumbents have 35–55 years of GBP age and thousands of reviews — gap takes time to close
  • Typical timeline: 6–12 months to enter top 5 in surrounding cities, 12–24 months for Greenville
THE TAKEAWAY
Your Google Business Profile + review engine wins this battle. Longer compound play — but unstoppable once it gets rolling.

The Top 5 Specific Factors

What we'll prioritize to climb the Map Pack.

Of the dozens of individual ranking factors inside Google Business Profile, these five carry the most weight for a local service business like yours:

  1. Primary GBP category set correctly. "HVAC Contractor" as primary; secondary categories enabled for AC repair, heating contractor, etc.
  2. Business name in GBP exactly matches reality. No keyword stuffing, no penalties.
  3. Quantity of native Google reviews + recency. Automated review requests via GoHighLevel feed this monthly.
  4. High-quality photos uploaded regularly. Vehicle wraps, job sites, team, equipment, before/after.
  5. Proximity of address to the searcher. Your physical Pickens location is fixed — this is why surrounding cities need their own location pages and GBP signals.
