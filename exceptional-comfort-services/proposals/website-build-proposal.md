## Website Build, SEO Optimization, and Lead Generation System Build-Out

### Discovery & SEO Evaluation

- Full SEO evaluation of the current website at https://www.exceptionalcomfortservices.com/
- Local keyword research for the Pickens, South Carolina service market
- Competitor SEO analysis for local HVAC competitors
- Identification of priority target keywords for local ranking
- Finalize the list of 11 new SEO-focused and content-positioning pages to be built (selected based on SEO evaluation, keyword opportunities, and Exceptional Comfort Services' service offerings)

### Existing Website Content Audit & Migration

- Full content extraction of the current Exceptional Comfort Services website
- Compile all current website content into a single master content document for review
- Audit existing content for SEO improvement opportunities
- Rewrite and optimize all existing pages with target local keywords as they are migrated to the new platform

### Website Build on GoHighLevel

- Build the new Exceptional Comfort Services website on the GoHighLevel platform
- Migrate and rewrite all existing pages from the current website
- Build 11 new pages identified during the discovery phase: 5 service pages, 5 location pages, and 1 content-positioning page for the Lake Keowee market
- Build a Privacy Policy page and a Terms and Conditions page (in addition to the 11 new pages)
- All page content optimized for target keywords, local search, and AI search visibility
- Mobile-responsive design
- On-page SEO setup: titles, meta descriptions, header structure, internal linking, image alt text, and schema markup where applicable
- Google Analytics integration: create or connect the Google Analytics property, install tracking on every page of the new website, configure baseline event tracking for form submissions and key interactions

### Domain Transfer & DNS Setup

- Coordinate the transfer of the existing domain (currently registered through Housecall Pro) to a registrar compatible with the new GoHighLevel website
- Configure DNS, point domain to GoHighLevel, and verify proper routing
- Setup SSL and email forwarding as needed

### Lead Capture Forms (Built in GoHighLevel)

- General "Contact Us" form
- System Installation Estimate signup form
- Form submissions stored as contacts in the GoHighLevel CRM
- Lead notification automations via email and SMS to designated team members
- Optional secondary lead delivery to a Google Sheet via n8n automation if desired

### Live Chat AI Agent (Built in GoHighLevel Conversation AI)

- Build a Conversation AI live chat agent inside GoHighLevel
- Train the agent's knowledge base on the completed website content, services, service area, hours, FAQ, and Exceptional Comfort Services' business information
- Configure conversation flows to capture lead information and qualify prospects
- Route qualified conversations into the GoHighLevel CRM as new leads

### Google Business Profile Integration

- Connect Exceptional Comfort Services' Google Business Profile to GoHighLevel
- Enable review monitoring and review request automation through GoHighLevel
- Enable Google Business Profile posting and post scheduling through GoHighLevel
- Train the Exceptional Comfort Services team on the GBP posting and review request workflows

### Team User Setup Inside GoHighLevel

- Add Exceptional Comfort Services team members as users inside the GoHighLevel subaccount
- Configure user roles and permissions for interacting with customers, conversations, and leads generated by the new website
- Provide brief platform walkthrough for each user role

### Phone Number Registration & Messaging Compliance

- Complete A2P 10DLC registration for Exceptional Comfort Services' phone number inside GoHighLevel so that SMS messages (lead follow-ups, automations, two-way texting) can be sent in compliance with carrier requirements
- Submit and manage Brand and Campaign registration through the GoHighLevel/Twilio A2P workflow
- Complete voice registration / caller ID setup (CNAM, Trust Hub verification, and SHAKEN/STIR-related attestation) so that outbound calls display proper business caller ID and avoid spam-labeling
- Coordinate any business documentation (EIN, business address, business website) required from Exceptional Comfort Services to complete carrier vetting

### Full Project Approval & Launch

- All website content, design, and assets are approved in writing by Exceptional Comfort Services before going live
- Final QA pass: links, forms, mobile rendering, page speed checks, SEO settings
- Domain cutover and launch live on GoHighLevel

### Final 11 New Pages List

Based on discovery and live SEO/keyword research for the Exceptional Comfort Services service area (Pickens, Greenville, Easley, Greer, Seneca, and the broader Upstate SC market), the following 11 pages will be built and optimized.

**Service pages (5):**

1. **AC Repair in Greenville, SC & The Upstate** — captures air conditioning repair commercial intent across the metro
2. **AC Installation & Replacement in The Upstate** — new system installs, including ductless mini-split applications
3. **Heating Repair & Furnace Repair in The Upstate** — heating-system service intent
4. **Heating Installation & Furnace Installation** — new system installs, including heat pump systems
5. **HVAC Maintenance & Tune-Up Plans** — supports recurring-revenue maintenance agreements

**Location pages (5):**

6. **HVAC Service in Greenville, SC** — primary anchor location page targeting the highest-volume HVAC keyword cluster in the service area
7. **HVAC Service in Easley, SC** — secondary anchor for the Easley market
8. **HVAC Service in Greer, SC** — service-area expansion city, low-difficulty ranking opportunity
9. **HVAC Service in Seneca, SC** — open ranking opportunity with very low keyword difficulty
10. **Service Areas Across Upstate SC** — hub page anchoring Pickens (home base) along with relevance blocks for Liberty, Powdersville, Six Mile, Sunset, Salem, Central, Clemson, Travelers Rest, Mauldin, Taylors, and Fountain Inn; each block links into the relevant service and primary location pages

**Content-positioning page (1):**

11. **Lake Home HVAC Services — Salem, Lake Keowee & The Upstate SC Lakes** — a content-positioning page covering lake-home HVAC service across Salem, Sunset, Walhalla, Westminster, Mountain Rest, Lake Keowee, and Lake Jocassee; designed to capture the high-value lake-home customer segment via referral conversion and AI search citation rather than traditional keyword volume

Plus the Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions pages already accounted for in this Exhibit.
