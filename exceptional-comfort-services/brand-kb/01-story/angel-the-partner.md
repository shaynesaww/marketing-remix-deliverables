## Who Angel is

Angel is Steven's wife and ECS's other half. On the May 18 onboarding call, when Shayne asked whether Angel should be on the website as part of the team, Steven turned to her and asked. She said yes.

She works a full-time job during the day as the office manager at Benjamin Franklin Plumbing in the area (a job we don't name on the website per the guardrails). But she's woven into ECS at every level.

## What she actually does

**Memory and operations.** Steven called her his brain — *"if I asked her, she was always my brain."* She remembers customer details by trigger words: she'll say one word and Steven will remember the whole story. She handles paperwork in the field. She supports him on calls.

**In the field on weekends and after hours.** On Easter Sunday, when ECS was on call, she was up on a ladder with Steven over a restaurant dining room handing him buckets of water with a rope while customers were eating below. They had fun doing it.

**On the phone.** When she answers, customers love her. Sometimes a customer will call and ask for Steven's direct line, and Angel will say, *"This is the company phone. This is his number."* The customer ends up loving her too.

**As the lead pipeline.** A meaningful share of ECS's calls come from people Angel encounters at her day job. (One day on the call, she mentioned two new ECS customers from a single day at her desk.) She'll likely go part-time at her day job eventually, but staying there has been worth it for the steady referrals and because they like the people.

## Why she's on the website

The brand is "family run, friendly feel, and exceptional quality." Angel is the family part. She's not a behind-the-scenes name; she's the half of the company that customers connect to before they even meet Steven.
