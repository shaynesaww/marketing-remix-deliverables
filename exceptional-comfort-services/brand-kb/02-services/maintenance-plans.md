## What ECS does

ECS offers scheduled maintenance agreements that cover the regular tune-ups, coil cleanings, filter changes, and system checks that keep a home's HVAC running properly across the seasons. Both standard residential maintenance and multi-system homes with more equipment to keep an eye on.

## Who it's for

Homeowners who'd rather have their system looked at on a schedule than wait for it to fail in July. Multi-system homes where keeping track of every unit on your own is unrealistic. Lake Keowee second homes where the owners aren't on-site and need somebody trustworthy keeping the equipment healthy. The first maintenance agreement Steven sold at ECS is a good example. A 10-system home in Sunset with a wine cellar, sold as a $3,000 plan, which has turned into roughly $20,000 of work since because the relationship is built on trust rather than a sales target.

## How Steven approaches it

Maintenance visits are where the small stuff lives. Most companies use them as a chance to upsell. Steven uses them as a chance to take care of the small stuff while he's already there. If he sees a section of line set that's missing insulation, he doesn't write up a $120 quote for Armaflex. He cuts a piece, tapes it on, shows the homeowner, and tells them it's handled.

The maintenance visit is also where Steven actually gets eyes on the system before something fails. That's the point of it. Caught early, most failures are inexpensive. Missed, they're expensive.

## What ECS won't do or claim

No using a maintenance visit as a sales appointment in disguise. No "we found this and you need to fix it today" pressure tactics. No nickel-and-diming for the small fixes that any honest tech would handle on the spot.
