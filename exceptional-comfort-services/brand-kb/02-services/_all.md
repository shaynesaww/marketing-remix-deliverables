Generator work is one of the areas where doing the job right and respecting the licensing rules matter at the same time. Steven holds the HVAC contractor's license. He does not hold those other two licenses, and he isn't going to pretend otherwise.

So the line is drawn cleanly. Service and repair on any generator, yes. Replacement of an existing generator in an existing footprint where the gas and electrical are already in place, yes. ## What ECS won't do or claim
