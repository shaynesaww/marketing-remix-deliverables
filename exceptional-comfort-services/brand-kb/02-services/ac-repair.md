## What ECS does

ECS diagnoses and repairs residential air conditioning systems across the Upstate. That covers everything from a unit that won't cool, to a system short-cycling, freezing up, leaking, or throwing a code. Steven works through the system the way it should be worked through, which means actually finding the cause before touching the wallet.

## Who it's for

Homeowners in Pickens, Easley, Greenville, Seneca, Greer, and the surrounding counties whose AC isn't doing what it should. Folks who want a real diagnosis instead of a sales pitch. People who have been burned before by a tech showing up, glancing at the unit for ten minutes, and quoting a new system.

## How Steven approaches it

Steven came up through 12 years of running service calls, eventually managing a 30-tech crew at one of the bigger players in town. He's seen every common failure and most of the uncommon ones. The story he told on the onboarding call sums up the approach. A friend's mom in Belton was told by another tech that her unit needed a TXV and a compressor. Steven knew that couldn't be right, because a unit with a bad compressor wouldn't be freezing up. He drove an hour out of his territory to look. The only thing wrong was a dirty filter. He cleaned it and left.

That's the model. Diagnose first. Fix what's actually broken. Charge what the work actually costs.

## What ECS won't do or claim

No fear language about a unit "failing." No quoting a replacement when a repair will hold. No charging $345 for a $20 capacitor. If a unit truly is past saving, Steven will say so and explain why, but that conversation is the exception, not the opener.
