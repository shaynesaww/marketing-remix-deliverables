## Bachelor's in HVAC

Steven holds a bachelor's degree in HVAC. Twelve years in the field on top of the formal training, working his way up from installer to service tech to service manager over a 30-tech crew before starting ECS.

## Why this matters

South Carolina law only requires one licensed contractor per HVAC company. That means a lot of trucks rolling around the Upstate are driven by techs who personally hold no license at all. ECS isn't built on that loophole. The owner is the license holder, and the owner is the one showing up at your house.
