## Certified Generac dealer

ECS is a certified Generac dealer for both service and installation. That covers warranty work on existing Generac units and full installations where the electrical and plumbing infrastructure is already in place.

A quick note on generator scope: South Carolina requires a separate plumbing license and a separate electrical license for new generator installs. ECS can service and replace existing Generac units, but for a brand-new install where gas and electrical have to be run from scratch, Steven brings in his brother-in-law who holds a master electrician's license and the customer pulls the permits. The Generac certification covers the HVAC side of that work.
