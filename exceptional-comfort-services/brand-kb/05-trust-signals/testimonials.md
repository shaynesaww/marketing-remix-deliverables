## Status

ECS does not have published testimonials collected and approved for the website yet. This is a Week 1 to Week 2 task in the post-launch sequence. The website launches with the founder story, customer stories (in the words of Steven, not the customer), and credentials. Real testimonials get layered in as they come in.

## Customers likely to give a strong testimonial

Based on the onboarding call, these are the relationships where Steven already has the kind of trust that turns into a written or video testimonial. The ask should come from Steven personally, not from a follow-up email.

- **Sam, the plumber at Benjamin Franklin** — has referred an estimated $30,000 to $50,000 in work, including his five rental properties and his own family. Will likely write a referral-style testimonial.
- **The Sunset / Lake Keowee customer** — 10-system home, $3,000 maintenance agreement, roughly $20,000 of work to date. High-trust relationship, premium-customer voice.
- **Allison's mom** — the Christmas no-heat fix. She already left a Google review. A short quote about being treated honestly when she didn't have money is gold.
- **The rental-property owner who only wanted Steven or his son** — loyalty story.
- **Janice** — the friend of Angel's overcharged thousands by two big shops on a misdiagnosed coil. Honest-pricing testimonial.

## Format to collect

Short written quote (2 to 3 sentences) with first name and town. Optionally a star rating. Photo if the customer offers. Video is a stretch goal.

## Where they live on the site

Homepage social proof block, About page, service pages relevant to the work performed.
