## Service area

The Upstate of South Carolina. Primary towns:

- Pickens
- Easley
- Greer
- Seneca
- Greenville
- Surrounding Oconee County and Pickens County
- **Lake Keowee** — yes (the Sunset / lake-home market). **Lake Jocassee** — no, too far.

## The ideal customer

Steven said it directly on the call: the house doesn't matter to him. A house is a house. What matters is **the conversation**.

His sweet spot:

- Homeowners who can hold a normal, down-to-earth conversation
- Middle-aged and older (skews better than younger — communication is more reliable)
- People who care about quality and don't need to be sold
- People who refer their friends, family, and neighbors
- Sam-the-plumber-type customers: quality-focused themselves, who only refer people they'd trust in their own house

## The premium tier — Lake Keowee

Lake Keowee customers have high-end second homes (one of Steven's customers has a $5.5M home there). Their expectations are different:

- Shoe covers on
- Trucks that don't leak oil ("their driveways probably cost more than my house")
- Presentation matters more
- Different equipment in play: fresh-air intakes, ERVs, dehumidifiers, steam humidifiers, sometimes geothermal

ECS serves geothermal **for maintenance and service**. Not for new installation (that gets reserved language on the website). But Steven will take a geothermal service call gladly — the word-of-mouth from that customer base is worth it.

## Customers ECS doesn't enjoy serving

Steven was honest about this:

- Belligerent or unsafe customers — see the Myrtle Beach walkaway story
- Customers who treat the trade like a commodity haggle
- Very low-income jobs — not because of the people (Steven's heartstrings get pulled and he ends up taking care of them anyway), but because they're financially painful for the business. Acknowledged but not advertised.

## Don't target on the website

- Apartment maintenance contracts (commercial-only operation isn't ECS's lane)
- Whole-house generator installations (no plumbing/electrical license — covered in services/generator-service.md)
- Anyone outside the listed service area
